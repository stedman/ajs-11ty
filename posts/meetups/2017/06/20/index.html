Meep! AustinJS is BACK! Meep Meep!

Here come dat beaker!

Hey friends! AustinJS is back with an exciting dual talk from Austin's own Tara Vancil and Paul Frazee about their project, [Beaker Browser][beaker], an experimental web browser with builtin hosting and p2p web protocols.

Meetup details
DATE June 20, 2017
TIME 7:30PM - 9:00PM
LOCATION Spredfast (200 West Cesar Chavez Street, Austin, TX 78701, 3rd Floor)

Afterwards, the discussion carries on at The Gingerman Austin (301 Lavaca Street, Austin, TX 78701).

Check back here or @AustinJS on Twitter for updates.

Speaker

Tara Vancil

Twitter

Tara helps build Beaker, a browser for the peer-to-peer Web. She’s enthusiastic about decentralizing the Web, and thinks that peer-to-peer protocols will reinvigorate the creativity of the Web’s early days.

Paul Frazee

Twitter

Paul works on Beaker, and is also very enthusiastic about a decentralized Web. He lives in Austin, TX.
